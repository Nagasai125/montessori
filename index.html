Our Playful Programs

Adventures in learning designed for every stage of early childhood.

Toddler (18-36 Months)

Fostering independence and "I can do it!" moments. Our toddlers learn practical life skills, language, and coordination in a safe, engaging environment.

Learn More →

Pre-Primary (18-36 Months)

A bridge between the toddler and primary years, focusing on social skills, concentration, and fine motor development in a prepared environment.

Learn More →

Primary (Years 3-6)

A world of discovery unfolds with hands-on materials for math, language, and science. Children work collaboratively and independently, building a strong academic foundation.

Learn More →

Kindergarten (Years 5-6)

The capstone year! Kindergarteners become leaders and mentors in the classroom, diving deep into projects that solidify their learning and prepare them for elementary school.

Learn More →

Lower Elementary (Years 6-9)

Students move from concrete to abstract learning, exploring advanced concepts in math, language, and cultural studies through research and collaborative projects.

Learn More →

Upper Elementary (Years 9-12)

Developing critical thinking and time-management skills, students take on complex, long-term projects and become responsible, self-directed learners.

Learn More →
